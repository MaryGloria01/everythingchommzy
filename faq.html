Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping with Everything Chommzy.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 3-7 business days depending on your location within Nigeria. Orders within Lagos are usually delivered within 1-3 business days. You'll receive tracking information once your order is shipped.

How much is shipping?

Delivery fees vary based on your location. Please contact us on WhatsApp with your delivery address and we'll provide you with the exact delivery cost to include in your payment.

Do you deliver outside Nigeria?

Currently, we only deliver within Nigeria. International shipping will be available soon. Please contact us via WhatsApp for special arrangements.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers to our verified bank accounts. After placing your order, you'll see our bank details and can upload your payment proof. We'll confirm your payment within 1-2 hours during business hours.

How do I use a promo code?

Enter your promo code in the "Enter promo code" field on the checkout page and click "Apply". The discount will be applied to your order total immediately.

Is my payment secure?

Yes! We use secure bank transfer methods and never store your banking details. All bank accounts displayed on our site are verified and belong to Everything Chommzy.

Products

Are your wigs made of real human hair?

Yes! Our premium wigs are made from 100% virgin human hair. They can be styled, colored, and heat-treated just like your natural hair. We also have synthetic options available at lower price points.

How do I choose the right wig size?

Most of our wigs come with adjustable straps and combs to fit most head sizes (21.5-22.5 inches circumference). If you need help choosing, contact us on WhatsApp with your head measurements.

What sizes are your clothes available in?

Our clothing ranges from Size S to XXL. Each product page shows available sizes. If you need help with sizing, check our size guide or message us on WhatsApp.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for unworn, unwashed items with original tags attached. Wigs that have been installed, cut, or styled cannot be returned. Contact us on WhatsApp to initiate a return.

How do I get a refund?

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method or as store credit.

What if I receive a damaged item?

Please contact us immediately via WhatsApp with photos of the damaged item. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund at no extra cost to you.

Account & Orders

How do I track my order?

Log in to your account and go to "My Orders" to see your order status. You'll also receive notifications when your order status changes.

Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order before payment is confirmed. Once confirmed, we begin processing immediately. Contact us on WhatsApp as soon as possible if you need to cancel.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page and enter your email address. You'll receive instructions to reset your password.

Still have questions?

Our customer support team is here to help!
